My Hobbies & Interests

Outside of work and school, I enjoy activities that keep me engaged, active, and creative. These hobbies reflect my personality and the way I approach challenges both personally and professionally.

  • Hunting – I enjoy hunting with my father, a tradition that’s helped me build patience, discipline, and focus. It’s also a great way to spend time outdoors. Learn more from the National Wildlife Federation.
  • Woodworking – I love designing and building furniture or home decor by hand. It allows me to be creative and build something from scratch. Explore ideas on Popular Woodworking.
  • Fitness & Staying Active – Whether it’s weightlifting, bodyweight workouts, or just going on hikes, staying fit helps me maintain balance and energy. I get tips and motivation from Bodybuilding.com.
  • Problem Solving – I like figuring out how things work or fixing broken tools or equipment. It’s a great way to apply critical thinking and learn something new with every challenge.
